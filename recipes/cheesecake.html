Cheescake Recipe

Cheesecake, a classic dessert adored for its creamy texture and luscious flavor, is a culinary masterpiece enjoyed worldwide. Crafted from a velvety mixture of cream cheese, sugar, and eggs, nestled atop a buttery graham cracker crust, it's a symphony of indulgence. Baked to perfection, its golden exterior gives way to a smooth, melt-in-your-mouth interior. Whether adorned with a decadent fruit compote or served plain, each forkful is a journey into blissful sweetness, making cheesecake an irresistible delight for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Fruit compote or topping of your choice (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter. Press mixture onto the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and 1 1/4 cups sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the mixture over the crust in the pan. Smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until the center is almost set.
  6. Remove from the oven and cool on a wire rack for 10 minutes. Loosen the edges with a knife. Let it cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Serve chilled, optionally topped with fruit compote or your favorite topping.

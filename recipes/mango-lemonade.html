Mango Lemonade Recipe

Mango Lemonade is a vibrant and refreshing drink that captures the essence of summer. Combining the tropical sweetness of ripe mangoes with the zesty tang of freshly squeezed lemon juice, it's a symphony of flavors that dances on the palate. With just the right balance of sweetness and tartness, each sip is a burst of sunshine, invigorating and revitalizing. Garnished with a sprig of mint or a slice of lemon, Mango Lemonade is the perfect companion for lazy afternoons or lively gatherings, offering a taste of paradise in every glass.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves or lemon slices for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a blender, combine diced mangoes, freshly squeezed lemon juice, and granulated sugar. Blend until smooth.
  2. Strain the mango-lemon mixture through a fine mesh sieve into a pitcher to remove any pulp.
  3. Stir in cold water, adjusting sweetness if needed.
  4. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the mango lemonade over the ice.
  5. Garnish with fresh mint leaves or lemon slices, if desired.
  6. Stir before serving and enjoy the refreshing taste of Mango Lemonade!
